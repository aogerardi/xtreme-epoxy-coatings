How it's done right

Prep is everything

Most coatings fail because of shortcuts on day one. We diamond-grind every slab — no acid wash, no peel.

01

Grind & Prep

Diamond-grind the concrete to a clean profile and vacuum it dust-free for a true mechanical bond.

02

Repair

Fill cracks, pits and spalls with structural patch so the finished floor reads flat and seamless.

03

Base & Flake

Roll the pigmented base coat and broadcast vinyl flake to full rejection in your chosen blend.

04

Top Coat & Seal

Lock it in with a clear polyaspartic top coat — UV-stable, chemical-resistant, walk-on the next day.
